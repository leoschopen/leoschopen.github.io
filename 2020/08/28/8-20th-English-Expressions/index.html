8.20th English Expressions

2020-08-28
  • English

  1. Nowadays, with the rapid development of our society, especially the IT and information industry, cell phones are accessible and they play a dominant role in our daily life, which greatly facilitate our lives.

  2. We as youngsters are expected to unleash the positive and become the backbone of the new times.年轻人要发扬正能量,做新时代的中坚力量。

  3. unleash the innovation 鼓励创新

  4. crave-want

  5. I still think that your idea is great and that you would turn it into a phenomenal success. .
    我仍认为你的想法非常棒，而且你会将它转变为巨大的成功。

  6. sensational/terrific -》good

  7. not important->immaterial

  8. support->be in favor of

  9. good eating habits help to fortify the body against disease

  10. It stands in sharp contrast to 与。。。形成鲜明对比

  11. Multiple presentation formats help most people learn.
    多元化展示形式有助于大多数人学习

    It actually seems that multiple presentation formats help most people learn.
    多元化展示形式似乎确实有助于大多数人学习

    It actually seems that multiple presentation formats,especially if one of them is visual, help most people learn.
    多元化展示形式似乎确实有助于大多数人学习，若其含有视觉形式则更是如此。

  12. the cultural or leisure pursuits that bring us happiness. 能给我们带来快乐的文化休闲活动

    pursuit 消遣

  13. brave the hardships 不畏艰难 brave the bitter cold 冒着严寒

  14. 砥砺前行 forge ahead

  15. inspire inspiration 激励鼓舞启发
