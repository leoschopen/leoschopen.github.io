    任何新的委员会都必须应对各种理论、经验和实际课题。

    ~ (yourself to) sth

    to think about a problem or a situation and decide how you are going to deal with it

    We must address ourselves to the problem of traffic pollution. 我们必须设法解决交通污染问题。

  37. 谴责Condemn sb to

    The government issued a statement condemning the killings.

    政府发表声明谴责这些凶杀事件。

    宣判 to say what sb’s punishment will be

    He was condemned to death for murder and later hanged.他因凶杀罪被判处死刑后被绞死。

    迫使…接受困境to force sb to accept an unpleasant situation

    They were condemned to a life of hardship.

    宣告使用…不安全

    表明有罪 to show or suggest that sb is guilty of sth

    She is condemned out of her own mouth (= her own words show that she is guilty) .

    她自己说的话显示她有罪责。

  38. non-stop adj adv

    直达的 without any stops a non-stop flight to Tokyo

    到东京的直达航班

    不间断的；不停的without any pauses or stops

    non-stop entertainment/work

  39. 奖学金 merit aid

  40. typically

    1,通常；一般used to say that sth usually happens in the way that you are stating

    The factory typically produces 500 chairs a week.这家工厂通常每周生产500把椅子。

    2，典型地in a way that shows the usual qualities or features of a particular type of person, thing or group

    typically American hospitality美国人特有的殷勤好客

    3,不出所料；果然in the way that you expect sb/sth to behave

    He was typically modest about his achievements.

    他一如既往，对自己的成就很谦虚。

  41. in a bid to 为了

    I come here in a bid to assess the situation. 对情况进行评定

    我到这里来是为了调查情况。

  42. 国有企业和事业单位state-owned enterprises and public institutions

    公立学校public institutions公营机构

  43. Unidentifiable无法辨认的; 难以确认的

  44. Object 宗旨；目的；目标 an aim or a purpose

    If you’re late, you’ll defeat the whole object of the exercise .如果你迟到了，便不能达到整个活动的目的。

  45. Objective 目标;目的 something that you are trying to achieve

    to meet/achieve your objectives

    object&objective

    object 强调个人或需求而决定的目标、目的。

    objective与object基本同义，但语义更广泛，指具体或很快能达到的目的，也可指军事目标。书面用词。

  46. objection反对

  47. Defeat 使失败；阻挠 to stop sth from being successful

  48. combat

    战斗

    防止；减轻to stop sth unpleasant or harmful from happening or from getting worse

    e.g measures to combat crime/inflation/unemployment/disease 防止犯罪 / 通货膨胀 / 失业 / 疾病的措施

  49. alleviation

    减轻；缓解If you alleviate pain, suffering, or an unpleasant condition, you make it less intense or severe.

    a great deal can be done to alleviate back pain.如今，减轻背部疼痛可以有许多方法。

    speeding up poverty alleviation 加快脱贫

    poverty alleviation 扶贫

  50. grinding

    无休止的If you describe a bad situation as grinding, you mean it never gets better, changes, or ends.

    Their grandfather had left his village in order to escape the grinding poverty.（贫苦不堪）为了摆脱贫困的煎熬，他们的祖父离开了村子。
