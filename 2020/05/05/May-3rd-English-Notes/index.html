Advanced Expression

1,good way/method –> the elixir of 灵丹妙药

persistence in reading and writing keeps the elixir of language learning

2，release the traffic jams –> alleviate

gain comfort from friends may alleviate our wounded heart

3, avoid an escalating trade war that makes economic engagement between China and the West impossible

避免不断升级的贸易战导致西方和中国之间的经济往来无法继续

4,try to do –>give sth a shot

5,don’t need –> dispense with sth //abandon

–>do away with //abolish,destroy

6,have much further to go 还有很长的路要走；距离目标还很远

Robot technology has much further to go if the machines are to replace human carers.要取代人类护工，机器人技术还有很长的路要走。

7,plenty of scope for sth 某事有发挥/增长空间

In the medium-term, economists say there is plenty of scope for China to maintain relatively high growth rates. 经济学家称，中期而言，中国保持相对高增长的空间很大。/room/place

8,sth is natural part of sth. …是…自然的一部分

Stress is a natural part of everyday life.压力是日常生活的一部分。

such churn is a natural part of rising prosperity这种剧烈变动是的走向富足一个必经之路

9,take a heavy toll on sth对…造成不好影响

Frequent move took a toll on children’s performance.经常搬家影响了孩子的成绩。
