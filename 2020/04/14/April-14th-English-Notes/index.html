April 14th English Notes

2020-04-14
  • English

words

1. greet - 对…作出反应 to react to sb/sth in a particular way - 映入…的眼帘to be the first thing that you see, hear or smell >Our help was greeted with appreciation. 受到了感谢
  1. prompt
    • 立即；迅速的；准时的 done without delay
    • 促使；导致 to be the first thing that you see, hear or smell
    • 提示，提醒，提词 to encourage sb to speak by asking them questions or suggesting words that they could say

      Prompt action was required as the fire spread.立即采取行动
      His speech prompted an angry outburst from a man激起了一男子的愤怒
      The program will prompt you to enter data where required.提醒你输入数据。

  2. popularization 普及
  3. engage
    • 吸引住（注意力、兴趣） to succeed in attracting and keeping sb’s attention and interest
    • 雇用；聘用 employ sb to do a particular job
    • engage in sth 从事，参加 engage in the activities

      It is a picturesque scenery place that engages us. 这是一个风景如画的地方，吸引了我们。
      He is currently engaged as a consultant.他现在受雇为顾问。

  4. justify 证实 ask for evidence to justify your bleak view
  5. sector 领域行业
  6. claim 宣称，索要， & proclaim 宣布，表明
    • to say that sth is true although it has not been proved and other people may not believe it
    • to publicly and officially tell people about sth important
  7. identical 相同的
  8. ideological 意识形态的
  9. whatsoever (用于名词词组后，加强否定陈述)丝毫，任何，无论什么

    There’s no reason whatsoever to turn down this suggestion.
    没有任何理由拒绝这个建议。

  10. back 支持 back up

phrases

1. could do with 需要

Teens could do with the edification and moralization of the traditional culture from the ancient poetry 青少年需要从古诗词中得到对传统文化的熏陶和教化。

  1. problems have been multiplying 成倍增加
