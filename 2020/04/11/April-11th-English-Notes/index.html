April 11th English Notes

2020-04-11
  • English

vocabulary

1,hypotheses n假说，假设;(凭空的)猜想

an idea which is suggested as a possible explanation for a particular situation or condition, but which has not yet been proved to be correct.
2,ideal n理想，完美的人或事
3,tempering n回火（炼钢）
tempering of the reality 现实的磨炼
4，argue 论证；说理
to give reasons why you think that sth is right/wrong, true/not true, etc., especially to persuade people that you are right
5,context 背景，上下文，语境
6，intervention 干涉;干预
7，recession 经济衰退;退后;撤回

the impact of the current recession on manufacturing
时下经济萎缩对制造业的影响

8,undertake 承担;从事;承诺
to make yourself responsible for sth and start doing it
to agree or promise that you will do sth

to undertake a task/project
He undertook to finish the job by Friday.
他答应星期五或之前完成这一工作。

9,slightly a little

I knew her slightly.

slender 苗条的;细的;窄的;微薄的;不足的

her slender figure 她苗条的身材

10,flourish v繁荣,昌盛,茁壮成长;健康幸福;(为引起注意)挥舞to develop quickly and be successful or common
n.令人难忘的方式;华丽辞藻an impressive act or way of doing sth

if our economy is to flourish 如果我们的经济要繁荣

11,succeed 接替；继任；随后出现

Who succeeded Kennedy as President?接替肯尼迪任总统的是谁？

12,elimate 消除

if the poverty is to be elimated 如果要消除贫困

13，well-being 健康幸福快乐

Singing can create a sense of wellbeing…唱歌能够给人一种心旷神怡的感觉。

14,Millennium [mɪˈleniəm] 一千年;千周年纪念日;千禧年
15,declaration 公告宣告 ~of a war; the Declaration of Independence
16,agency a government department that provides a particular service
17,gathering n.聚集;聚会;集会;收集v.聚集;集合;召集;收拢(分散的东西);搜集

the twenty-second annual gathering of the South Pacific Forum.第22届南太平洋论坛年会

18,indicator 指示信号;迹象;指示器;指针;方向灯
a sign that shows you what sth is like or how a situation is changing

The economic indicators are better than expected.经济指标比预期的好。

19,tease v.取笑;戏弄;挑逗，撩拨(异性)n.爱戏弄人的人;逗弄者;取笑者;戏弄;捉弄;勾引人者

tease out 套出，巧妙获得（信息或答案）
he was sure he could tease it out if only he had time. 他肯定，只要他有时间就一定能弄个清楚。
tease out the answers 套出答案

20,degradation 堕落;潦倒(的境况);a situation in which sb has lost all self-respect and the respect of other people 毁坏，恶化(过程)the process of something becoming worse or weaker, or being made worse or weaker.

the degradation of being sent to prison 被关进监狱的落泊境况
environmental degradation 环境恶化

21,derive v.获得;(使)起源;(使)产生
derive pleasure from helping others 助人为乐
be derived from sth 起源于；来自

‘politics’ is derived from a Greek word ‘politics’一词源自希腊语

22,propotion 部分;比例;倍数关系;均衡

a large proportion of 一大部分
The proportion of men to women 男女比例

23, grind 磨碎;磨快;磨光n.令人疲劳(或厌倦)的工作;苦差事;刺耳的摩擦声

to grind coffee/corn将咖啡 / 谷粒磨成粉

24,case n具体情况;事例;事实;特殊情况;案件；容器;论据
a particular situation or a situation of a particular type

In some cases 在某些情况下
the true situation
If that is the case (= if the situation described is true) 如果真是那样
a situation that relates to a particular person or thing
In your case, we are prepared to be lenient.根据你的情况，我们可以从宽处理。
a murder case 案件
the case for the defence/prosecution 有利于被告 / 原告的论据

25,graphic 图案的;形象的，生动的，逼真的(尤指令人不快的事物) 图表
very clear and full of details, especially about sth unpleasant

a graphic account/description of a battle 对战斗的生动叙述 / 描述
He kept telling us about his operation, in the most graphic detail .
他不停地向我们绘声绘色地讲述他动手术的详细情况。

26,discernible 看得清的;辨别得出的you can see it or recognize that it exists

Far away the outline of the island is just discernible…远处，岛屿轮廓仅依稀可辨。

27,across
across our diverse planet 在我们这个多元化的星球上
across a dozens of environment issue 在许多环境问题上
28,improvement 改善，改进

phrases

>1,Residence among the poor **is suggested as** a way of understanding their problems. >有人提出与穷人同住是了解他们问题的一种办法。

2,sow the seeds

I sow the seed in pots of soil-based compost. 我把种子种在几盆掺有堆肥的土里。

3,grind to a halt 逐渐停止;慢慢瘫痪;（车辆）声音刺耳地缓缓停下

In winter they almost grind to a halt. 到了冬天，它们会停止活动。

He cites capacitors and connectors, without which much of the modern world would grind to a halt. 他举了电容器和连接器的例子，没有这两样产品，现代世界将大面积陷入停顿。

4,better off 境况（尤指经济性）较好的

He’s much better off than before. 他手头比过去宽多了。
