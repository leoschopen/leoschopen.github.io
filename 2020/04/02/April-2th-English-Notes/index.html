Posted on 2020-04-02

4.1

1，adhere 黏附; 附着 adhere to sth (formal) 坚持，遵守，遵循（法律、规章、指示、信念等）

For ten months he adhered to a strict no-fat low-salt diet.十个月来他严格坚持无脂肪少盐饮食。

2，dosage 剂量 a high dosage of vitamin C

3，practitioner (尤指医学或法律界的)从业人员;习艺者;专门人才

dental practitioners 牙医

4，otherwise 否则，除此以外

We’re committed to the project. We wouldn&#39;t be here otherwise.否则我们就不会到这里来了

5，encounter 遭遇，遇到，邂逅 encountered such resistance 遇到过这么大的阻力

6，be at risk for you may be at risk for heart disease 你可能有心脏病的风险

7，Samoa 萨摩亚群岛（太平洋）

11,remedy 处理方法; 疗法,药品; 纠正

no simple remedy for unemployment 失业问题没有简单的解决办法

a herbal remedy 草药 to remedy a problem 解决问题

12，hazard n危险；v冒失提出，冒险猜测；（to make a suggestion or guess which you know may be wrong）冒…的风险

a fire/safety hazard 火灾 / 安全隐患

Would you like to hazard a guess ? 你想猜猜看吗？

hazard other people's lives as well as their own 拿他人和自己的生命冒险

"有关增加的英文表述"

（1）increase， rise，grow up,beef up等词，当表示"激增、猛增、大幅增加"时，还有像       surge，soar，mount，climb，proliferate等表达方式**
（2） 例句：Alibaba is also planning to **beef up ** what it calls "ET Medical Brain"加强
As such deals  **proliferated** , economists studied their impact.随着这类协定的激增、
its quarterly results included a  **surge ** in revenues of 15%它的季报显示收入激增15%
Business education is enjoying  **a surge of**  popularity in Ukraine 在乌克兰的受欢迎     程度激增
people with sight problems will  **soar**  in the coming decades有视力问题的人数将会激     增
As costs  **skyrocket** 随着开销猛增 prices  **skyrocket**  by more than 2,000%

"举行的英文表达"

  1. （1）hold,give,stage,host

  2. （2）be held [taken place] as scheduled 某事被"举行",用sth is held by sb 或者sth is taken place in someplace

    stage a strike 举行罢工    stage an uprising;举行起义

give a banquet  举行宴会
